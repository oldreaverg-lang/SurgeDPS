Section 0: Version History
v1 — Original Exponential Depth Model
MAPE 63%, 2/6 in range, no wind. Initial baseline with exponential depth parameterization; significant overestimation of surge.
v2 — Rayleigh Depth + Phase 1 Wind
MAPE 20%, 4/6 in range, Wind MAPE 99%. Rayleigh depth distribution + basic wind component introduced; significant improvement but wind component unstable.
v3 — Asymmetric Holland Wind Fields
Gust factor 1.25, interaction coefficient 0.50. MAPE 25.4%, 3/6 in range, Wind MAPE 52.3%, 3/6 wind ±50%. Regression from v2 — over-aggressive gust and interaction parameters over-counted wind damage and reduced overall accuracy.
v4 — Calibrated Wind Parameters (Current)
Gust factor tuned to 1.15, interaction reduced to 0.30 via 225-combination parameter sweep. MAPE 16.0%, 5/6 in range, Wind MAPE 28.9%, 5/6 wind ±50%. Matches v2 aspirational accuracy on combined losses while achieving substantial wind component calibration. Harvey (-47%) remains holdout — rain-dominated event outside model scope.
