Section 1: v5 Enhancement Modules

RS Means Property Valuation

Replaces flat $150/sqft with RS Means regional cost indices for all 50 states + PR. Granular per-HAZUS-type costs ($85-$195/sqft national baseline), year-built depreciation (0.75-1.12), per-building hash variation (0.75-1.25). Outputs (low, mid, high) range for uncertainty quantification.

Probabilistic Foundation Estimation

NFIP compliance era model (pre-1973: 10% elevated, 2005+: 85%). FEMA flood zone requirements (V-zone: 5-12ft, AE: conditional). Regional construction patterns for 13 states. Outputs best_estimate_ft, prob_elevated, and confidence level.

Parametric TC Rainfall (Lonfat 2004)

Rain rate ~ max_wind^0.6 × exp(-distance/r_scale) with quadrant asymmetry. Slowdown factor for stalled storms (Harvey-type). Ponding factor = 0.30 (conservative). Compound damage = max(surge_loss, rainfall_loss) per building.

Composite Confidence Scoring

Weighted score: value quality (35%) + foundation quality (30%) + depth quality (20%) + wind data (15%). Three tiers: high (≥0.65), medium (≥0.35), low (<0.35). Per-building transparency for adjuster review.
